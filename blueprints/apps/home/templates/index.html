Welcome to the Cat Identifier

The Cat Identifier has been developed to allow breeders or pet owners to find out the phenotype of their British shorthair cats by uploading an image of the cat. The application uses machine learning models to detect the cat's phenotype from the image.

If you are a breeder or pet owner, then you can use the Breeder App to find out your cat's phenotype. However, please be aware that - depending on how well trained our model is at this point - your prediction may not always be accurate. Please be assured that our administrators are working hard to bring you the very best predictions that technology allows.

The Cat Identifier also provides a set of tools that allows model administrators to manage the machine learning model. This helps to ensure that breeders and pet owners get good quality predictions about their cat every time.
